Installing Applications

Now that you have internet connection, you can setup some of the applications you want on your computer. I will show you multiple ways of installing the applications you want. I will show you how to setup three applications as examples. I chose Netlfix so I can watch movies and TV shows on my computer and McAfee so that my computer would be protected against viruses.

Installing McAfee (using the website)

  • Go to google and search 'McAfee', click the first website you see which should say 'McAfee.com - McAfeeÂ® Official Site'
  • On the website, chose the package you wish to select by pressing the blue button that says 'Buy Now'
  • Once you have selected the package you want, fill in the needed information under Account and Billing Info
  • Once you have filled in the needed information click the 'Agree and Continue' button
  • Now just follow the on-screen instructions to finish installing McAfee

Installing Netflix (using the Windows store)

  • Click the Windows button on your keyboard and search 'store'
  • In the store go to the search bar and search 'Netflix'
  • Once you are on the Netflix page, click the green button that says Install. The app will now install
  • To open the application click the windows button on your keyboard and search for the application you have downloaded
