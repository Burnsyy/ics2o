Installing Applications

Now that you have internet connection, you can set up some of the applications you want on your computer. I will show you multiple ways of installing the applications you want. I will show you how to setup two applications as examples. I choose Netflix so I can watch movies and TV shows on my computer and McAfee so that my computer would be protected against viruses.

Installing McAfee (using the website)

  • Go to google and search 'https://www.mcafee.com' in the navigation bar
  • On the website, choose 'For Consumers' under the 'Select Your Security Solution' bar
  • On this page, scroll down and pick the package you want based on your needs and select 'Buy Now' to purchase that product
  • Once you have selected the package you want, fill in the needed information under Account and Billing Info
  • Once you have filled in the needed information click the 'Agree and Continue' button
  • Now complete the McAfee installation wizard by agreeing to the term of service and selecting where you would like the McAfee program to be in your computer
  • Now click 'Install', you now have McAfee Virus Protection on your computer

Installing Netflix (using the Windows store)

  • Click the Windows button on your keyboard and search 'store'
  • In the store go to the search bar and search 'Netflix'
  • Once you are on the Netflix page, click the green button that says Install. The app will now install
  • To open the application click the windows button on your keyboard and search for the application you have downloaded
