Using the Mouse and keyboard

I have created a drawing program using the mouse and keyboard. You can change the colour by:

  • Pressing 'B' to add blue and 'V' to take away blue from the colour
  • Pressing 'R' to add red and 'E' to take away red from the colour
  • Pressing 'G' to add green and 'F' to take away green from the colour
  • Pressing the space bar to reset the colour

you can also change the background by clicking the numbers 0-9 on the numpad:

  1. White
  2. Black
  3. Blue
  4. Red
  5. Orange
  6. Yellow
  7. Green
  8. Purple
  9. Pink
  10. (0) Gray
