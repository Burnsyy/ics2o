Summative Video Game

  • You start in a menu and can choose to go to either the game, the instructions, or the settings
  • The instructions will tell you how to play/li>
  • The settings will let you customize your level, player colour, and whether or not the sound is on
  • The goal of the game is to last as long as you can without dying
  • The score will go up for the time you are alive
  • To move change the gravity you press 'w' on the keyboard
  • Everytime you hit an object you will lose one life
  • The objects will be squares and you will be a circle
