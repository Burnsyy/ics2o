What the Government Does to Prevent it

There are several laws within the criminal code of Canada which make it illegal for people to hack, these laws inclue;

  • Section 184 - which deals with privacy
  • Section 342 - which dealds with theft, forgery of credit cards and unauthorized uses of computers
  • Section 402 - which deals with identity theft
  • Section 403 - which deals with identity fraud


These laws are made in hopes to try and stop criminals from using hacking as a way to obtain information that isn't theirs.
