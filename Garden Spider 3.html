Transfering Information

Now that you have internet connection and some applications, you now need to transfer your information from your old computer to your new computer. You also need to setup some of your peripherals like a wireless printer.

Tranfering Information on a Home Network

  • Click on the Windows button and serach for "HomeGroup"
  • Once you are on the homegroup screen, click 'Creat a homegrou', then click next
  • You can now select the prefrences you want for your homescreen. Once you have picked the settings you like, click 'Next'
  • Now setup a password for your homegroup. Once you are done that click 'Finish'. You have now created your own homegroup!
  • Inorder to join the homegroup from other computers, you have to go to the HomeGroup page and click on the created group then enter the password.
  • To share files in your homegroup, right click on the file you want to share and click 'Share with', then click either the 'Homegroup (view)' button which allows your homegroup only to view the file, or the 'Homegroup (view and edit)' button which allows your homegroup to edit your file.
  • Now you can share as many files between computers as you would like!

Tranfering Information Privatally Using an USB

  • Plug your USB into your computer
  • Then open 'file explorer' by clicking the windows button on your keyboard and searching 'file explorer'

Setting up a Wireless Printer

  • Click the windows button on your keyboard and search 'devices and printers'
  • Click 'Add a printer'
  • Select your printer in the menu with all the local printers
  • If needed, install the printer driver by clicking 'install driver'
  • Complete the on-screen instructions then press 'Finsih'
  • You now have a wireless printer connected to your computer
