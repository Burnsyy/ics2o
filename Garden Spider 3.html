Transfering Information

Now that you have internet connection and some applications, you now need to transfer your information from your old computer to your new computer. You also need to setup some of your peripherals like a wireless printer.

Tranfering Information Publically

  • Logon to github or create an account
  • On the home page of github (https://github.com) click 'start a project'
  • Then create a repository name of your choice and make sure the repository is public
  • Now click 'Create repository'
  • Now go to settings at the top right of your tabs on the website
  • Next scroll down to 'Theme chooser', select 'choose a theme', and select a theme for your repository
  • Once you have selected a theme, go back to code in your repository and select 'upload files'
  • Drag any files you want to be public into the designated area and select commit changes
  • These files will now be public to the rest of the world!

Tranfering Information Privatally Using an USB

  • Plug your USB into your computer
  • Then open 'file explorer' by clicking the windows button on your keyboard and searching 'file explorer'
  • Click on your USB stick to open the file containing everything on your USB
  • Now find all of the files you want to put on your USB by going to file explorer and dragging any files you want into your USB file
  • Once you have obtained all of the files you want and they have loaded onto your usb, click the USB image that is on the bottom right of your screen and select 'eject'

Setting up a Wireless Printer

  • Click the windows button on your keyboard and search 'devices and printers'
  • Click 'Add a printer'
  • Select your printer in the menu with all the local printers
  • If needed, install the printer driver by clicking 'install driver'
  • Complete the on-screen instructions then press 'Finsih'
  • You now have a wireless printer connected to your computer
